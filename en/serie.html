A remarkable serie "Vincenzo"

Vincenzo (빈센조) is a South Korean television serie that falls under the category of dramas. This serie was directed by Kim Hee Won, was scripted by Park Jae-bum and has a season of 20 episodes of about one hour.
It was first broadcast in South Korea on tvN channel from February 20, 2021, and then on the streaming platform Netflix for the rest of the world.

The synopsis of the serie

At the age of eight, Park Joo-hyung, a Korean orphan, is taken in by Don Fabio, the head of the Cassano mafia. He will receive all the necessary teachings and will become in adulthood Vincenzo Cassano, the lawyer and consigliere of the Cassano family.

However, after Fabio's death, Pablo (his biological son), claims total control of the mafia. And to achieve his goals, he tries to assassinate Vincenzo. Vincenzo, rejected, will fly to Seoul to solve the last case he has left.
In the past, Vincenzo had helped a powerful businessman hide his gold in a secure vault buried under a building in Seoul, the Geumga-dong Plaza. However, this man is now dead, and Vincenzo intends to use this gold, which nobody knows about.

Unfortunately, a big problem will arise: Babel, a Korean giant with several subsidiaries (Babel Pharmaceutics, Automobiles, ..) is threatening to buy Geumga-dong Plaza and raze it to the ground to build new premises for its employees. Vincenzo will have to use his skills as a lawyer (and as a mafioso) to save the Geumga-dong Plaza, its tenants and above all, to recover his gold!

The author's point of view...

My passion for kDramas has been on the rise lately! So, when I discovered the Vincenzo serie, I found myself in a universe that I like and that includes: mafia, investigations and trials. The endearing characters have more than seduced me and I could realize the power and influence on people, that wealth confers.
