Filmography of
Kim Hee Won

The director

Kim Hee Won has directed several films and series during her career before she released the serie Vincenzo. She still continues to produce.

Her works

"Come, Come, Absolutely Come"

Serie released on December 5, 2011

1 season, 60 episodes

Synopsis:

We will follow the crazy stories of two women: Bae Soo-Jin and Kim Sae-Bom, living under the same roof as a man: Ko Chan-Young.

"Come, Come, Absolutely Come"

"You Are My Destiny"

Serie released on July 2, 2014

1 season, 20 episodes

Synopsis:

Lee Gun, CEO of a large company and Kim Mi-Young, a simple employee will find themselves, by accident, spending a night together. It is only a few weeks later that Mi-Young will learn of her pregnancy. Coming from two totally different worlds, our two characters will have to resolve to get married: for better, and for worse!

"You Are My Destiny"

"Old Goodbye"

Film released on November 9, 2014

Synopsis:

Soo-Hyuk, a former boxer, learns that his ex-wife is seriously ill. A few days later, while cleaning up his house, he finds a camera that she was very attached of...

"Old Goodbye"

"Money Flower"

Serie released November 11, 2017

1 season, 24 episodes

Synopsis:

To save his family inheritance, a powerful man will force his best lawyer, who incidentally serves as his henchman, to marry the daughter of a rich politician. Unfortunately for him, not everything goes according to the plan...

"Money Flower"

"The Crowned Clown"

Serie released on January 7, 2019

1 season, 16 episodes

Synopsis:

To escape persecution and the claims of the people, a king puts in power a "clown" who looks just like him. However, the clown will take his role very much to heart: he will get closer to the queen and the people and will do everything to be the best of the sovereigns!
